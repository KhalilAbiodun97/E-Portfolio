About CompTIA Security+

CompTIA Security+ is a globally recognised certification that validates essential cybersecurity skills. It covers topics such as threats, attacks, vulnerabilities, security tools, secure network design, identity and access management, risk management, and cryptography. Ideal for roles like Security Administrator and Network Administrator, this certification serves as a foundational credential for those pursuing a career in cybersecurity. It is widely recognised by employers and often required for entry-level IT security positions.
