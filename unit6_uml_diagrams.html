Unit 6: UML Diagrams for E-Commerce Site

UML Diagrams

In Unit 6, I worked on creating UML diagrams to design an e-commerce shopping site. These diagrams help in visualizing the architecture and behavior of the system. Below are the diagrams I created:

1. Activity Diagram

The Activity Diagram represents the workflow of the e-commerce site, from the customer's interaction to the backend processing.

2. Class Diagram

The Class Diagram shows the structure of the system in terms of classes and relationships between them. It defines the core components like Customers, Orders, and Products.

3. Sequence Diagram

The Sequence Diagram illustrates the sequence of interactions between the customer and the system, particularly focusing on the order process.
